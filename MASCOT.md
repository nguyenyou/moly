# Mascot Prompt

Use this prompt with an AI image generator to create the moly mascot.

## Background

In Homer's Odyssey, **moly** is a magical herb with a **white flower** and a **black root**. Hermes gave it to Odysseus to protect him from Circe's transformation spell — she turned men into pigs, but moly made Odysseus immune. The herb represents protection from unnecessary transformation, which is exactly what this library does: it protects your data from being forced through an unnecessary JSON AST transformation.

## Prompt

A friendly, minimal mascot for a software library called "moly". The mascot is a small, cheerful character — a white flower with a glowing golden center and a short black root, inspired by the magical herb "moly" from Greek mythology. The flower has a simple, cute face with confident eyes and a subtle smile, giving it a calm and protective personality.

Style: flat vector illustration, clean lines, modern and minimal. Suitable for use as a small logo, GitHub avatar, and documentation icon. White and gold flower, black root, on a transparent or dark background. No text. Think mascots like Go's gopher or Rust's Ferris crab — simple, iconic, instantly recognizable at any size.

Personality: calm, fast, protective. Not aggressive — more like a quiet guardian that simply makes bad things not happen.

## Variations to try

- **Simple**: just the flower, no face, abstract and geometric
- **Character**: the flower with a face, standing confidently
- **Action**: the flower standing between a stream of data (bytes on one side, a case class on the other), with a JSON tree fading away behind it
- **Icon**: extreme minimal — just the white petals and gold center, works at 16x16 favicon size
